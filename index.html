Describe a difficult challenge you met.

词汇储备

  • from scratch
  • Unity engine
  • steep learning curve
  • debugging
  • countless hours
  • persevered
  • sense of pride
1. 开头：点明挑战"I'd like to talk about a recent challenge I faced, which was creating a 2D game from scratch for my graduation application."
2. 过程：你如何应对？"I had to learn everything, from coding to art design. There were times I got stuck on a bug and felt like giving up, but I persevered by watching tutorials."
Describe a time when you were very busy.

词汇储备

  • hectic schedule
  • swamped with work
  • tight deadline
  • prioritize tasks
  • drained
  • sense of achievement
思路引导谈论期末周或大项目截止前。描述你是如何制作时间表 (Schedule) 并一件件完成任务的，最后感到一种巨大的解脱 (relief)。
